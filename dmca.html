DMCA Copyright Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

WishaImage respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects its users to do the same. We comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and other applicable copyright laws.

Important: WishaImage is a platform that hosts user-submitted content. We respond promptly to valid DMCA takedown notices and have a process for addressing copyright infringement claims.

1. Our Copyright Policy

WishaImage operates under the following copyright principles:

  • We respect the intellectual property rights of all content creators
  • We respond to valid DMCA takedown notices within 24-48 hours
  • We maintain a transparent process for copyright disputes
  • We provide a counter-notification process for users
  • We terminate repeat infringers in appropriate circumstances

2. Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe your copyrighted work has been copied and is accessible on WishaImage in a way that constitutes copyright infringement, you may notify us by providing the following information:

Required Information for DMCA Takedown Notice:

  1. Identification: Your full name, address, telephone number, and email address
  2. Description: A description of the copyrighted work that you claim has been infringed
  3. URL(s): The exact URL(s) of the allegedly infringing content on our website
  4. Ownership Statement: A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law
  5. Accuracy Statement: A statement under penalty of perjury that the information in your notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on the copyright owner's behalf
  6. Signature: Your electronic or physical signature

What Happens After You Submit a DMCA Notice:

Step Action Timeline
1 We review your DMCA notice for completeness Within 24 hours
2 If valid, we remove or disable access to the infringing content Within 48 hours
3 We notify the content uploader about the takedown Within 24 hours of removal
4 We maintain records of the takedown notice As required by law

Warning: Knowingly misrepresenting that material is infringing may subject you to liability for damages (including costs and attorneys' fees) under Section 512(f) of the DMCA.

3. Counter-Notification Process

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notification containing:

  • Your contact information
  • Identification of the removed content and its location before removal
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you believe the content was removed by mistake
  • Consent to jurisdiction in federal court if your address is outside the US
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Counter-Notification Timeline:

  • We will forward your counter-notification to the original complainant
  • The complainant has 10 business days to file a lawsuit
  • If no lawsuit is filed, we may restore the content after 10-14 business days

4. Repeat Infringer Policy

In accordance with the DMCA and other applicable laws, WishaImage has adopted a policy of terminating, in appropriate circumstances, users who are deemed to be repeat infringers. We may also at our sole discretion limit access to the service and/or terminate the accounts of any users who infringe any intellectual property rights of others, whether or not there is any repeat infringement.

Our Three-Strike Policy:

  • First Strike: Warning and content removal
  • Second Strike: Temporary suspension (7-30 days)
  • Third Strike: Permanent account termination

6. Fair Use Considerations

Before submitting a DMCA notice, please consider whether fair use, fair dealing, or a similar exception to copyright applies. Common fair use cases include:

  • Criticism or commentary
  • News reporting
  • Teaching and research
  • Parody or satire

Note: We are not legal experts and cannot determine whether a particular use qualifies as fair use. We process DMCA notices based on the information provided and applicable law.

7. International Copyright Considerations

WishaImage operates globally and respects international copyright laws. While we primarily follow the U.S. DMCA process, we also consider:

  • EU Copyright Directive
  • Berne Convention requirements
  • Local copyright laws in users' jurisdictions

8. Policy Updates

We may update this DMCA Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
